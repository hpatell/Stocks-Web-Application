stocks4me Tutorial


Getting Started

Go to the User Profile page by clicking on your profile picture or wallet amount on the right side of the navigation bar.

Here, you can update your wallet amount or sign out. Enter an amount of money in your wallet; this will be used to buy and sell stocks.

The Homepage

The "Home" tab is where you can search for stocks, view all the stocks in your portfolio, read news stories, and buy/sell shares.

To buy or sell shares, you must search the stock first. Try searching for "Amazon"!

You can now see Amazon's current information, updating in realtime. To buy a share, use the "Buy Share" button at the bottom of the search results.

You now have a few shares of Amazon stock. Congratulations! If you scroll down, you will see the most recent information for all your stocks, in addition to news stories.

Stocks in your portfolio are updated whenever you sign in. To refresh manually, use the "View Realtime Info" button.

The Dashboard

The dashboard is a planning tool that allows you to view all your stocks on the same graph, as well as see your total portfolio value.

On the right, you'll see a list of your stocks. You can show or hide any stock you like, which will be updated in the graph.

That's It!

You're now ready to use stocks4me to plan your investments.

If you need a refresher, you can view this tutorial anytime from the "About" page.
